We send invoices at the end of the month for the previous time period. These invoices are due within 2 weeks and we take payment via e-transfer to Clinton@LargeTutoring.com. We also accept payment via credit card on the invoice, however, e-transfer is the preferred method, if possible.

There is a late fee of 10% per month on all invoices that we have not received payment for past the due date. We do our best to send several reminders, and we know different families have different financial situations. We are flexible and will do our best to avoid late fees - reach out and stay in contact in these situations.
